Our Story

About God's Covenant Hospital

On February 1, 2001 — 25 years ago — God's Covenant Hospital began its journey with a simple but powerful purpose: to provide reliable, ethical, and compassionate healthcare to the Lagos community. Since then, we have grown to become one of the most trusted healthcare facilities in Oworonsoki, serving over 28,000 patients and delivering over 2,250 babies safely.

We believe every patient deserves quality medical attention delivered with dignity, respect, and empathy — regardless of their economic background.

Our hospital blends modern medical practices with a deeply human approach to care. Whether you are visiting for routine consultations, maternity care, or emergency treatment, our focus remains your safety, comfort, and long-term wellbeing.

We are accredited by HEFAMAA and NHIA, and we partner with 7 leading HMO providers to ensure that our services are accessible through health insurance.

Mission & Vision

What Drives Us

Our Vision

OUR VISION is to be a foremost healthcare facility with high standard and evidence-based medical practice, contributing significantly to the reduction in the overall child and maternal morbidity and mortality, both in our immediate environment and in the global community at large.

Our Mission

OUR MISSION is to provide high quality healthcare, using experienced, dedicated, enthusiastic, and properly cultured staff; well-groomed for the fear of God in all their dealings and showing love, sympathy and empathy to all categories of patients.
