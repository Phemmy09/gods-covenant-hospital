Our Medical Services

Comprehensive, patient-centred healthcare at every stage of life — from antenatal to emergency care and beyond.

01

Antenatal & Delivery Care

Our maternity services support women throughout pregnancy, delivery, and postnatal recovery in a safe and caring environment.

  • Antenatal Clinics — Every Tuesday, 7:30am – 12:00pm
  • Normal and assisted deliveries
  • Elective and emergency caesarean deliveries
  • Postnatal care and support
  • Ultrasound scan for all antenatal patients at every visit
02

Immunization & Childcare

We provide essential healthcare services to infants and children to promote healthy growth and development.

  • Routine immunizations per national schedule
  • Child wellness checks
  • Pediatric consultations
  • Growth monitoring
03

Surgical Services

We offer selected minor and general surgical procedures performed by qualified professionals in a clean, safe environment.

  • Minor surgeries
  • Pre- and post-operative care
04

Family Planning

We help individuals and families make informed reproductive health decisions with professional guidance and support.

  • Contraceptive counseling
  • Multiple birth control options
05

General Medical Services

Daily consultation, emergency care, and treatment for all patients. Our medical doctors are always on duty.

  • Daily Consultation: 9am – 2pm & 6pm – 9pm (Monday to Sunday)
  • Emergency Consultations: 24 hours a day
  • Permanent Night Duty Coverage by Medical Doctors
  • Clean, ambient environment without hospital odour
06

Laboratory & Ultrasound Scan Services

Accurate clinical diagnosis with instant results from our on-site laboratory, and comprehensive ultrasound scan services.

  • On-site laboratory for accurate clinical diagnosis
  • Instant results
  • Ultrasound scan services for all patients
